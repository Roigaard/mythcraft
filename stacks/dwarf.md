# Dwarf

At level 1, you gain the following features as a dwarf. You gain additional features based on your sublineage.

| Feature        | Description                                                                                                                                            |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Stonesense** | You have a natural affinity for caving and the underground world. Gain +5 to any History, Geography, and Navigating* checks regarding the underground. |
| **Darkvision** | You can see in darkness to a limit, even without any light source. Gain 30 ft of Darkvision.                                                           |
