| **Mage Entry** | Character Level 2 | Gain **Learned:** Gain +6 Skill Points for any INT-tagged skills.<br>Gain **Student of the Arcane:** (see Arcane talent track) if you don’t already have it: gain +5 SP and +1 Arcane Power instead. ## Focus

| **Alchemy**    | Spend 10 minutes reshaping raw materials to your needs. The creation cannot exceed 1 ft × 1 ft × 2 ft. Materials may be refined (smelted ore, cut timber) but not fully crafted (sword, door). Silver, gold, and diamond cannot be transmuted. |
