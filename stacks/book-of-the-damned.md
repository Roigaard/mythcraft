## Prayers

* **Gracefull poise** (Divine Prayer, Enchanting); APC 2; Range 20'; Requires: Verbal, Visual; Duration: 1 round
  * Effect: One creature of your choice gains Immune Charmed and Immune Enthralled. This does not work if the creature is already Charmed or Enthralled.
  * Divine power: 3 (+20' range), 7/12/16/24(+40' range each)
* **Sacred flare** (Divine Prayer, Evoking); APC 2; Range 60'; Requires: Gesture, Verbal, Visual, line of effect; Duration: instantaneous
  * Effect: Blast a creature with a divine flare of energy. Make a magic attack against the creature’s REF. On a hit, deal 1d8 radiant damage.
  * Divine Power: 3/7/12/16 (+20' range each), 24 (+1d8 damage)
