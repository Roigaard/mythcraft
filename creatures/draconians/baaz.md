# Draconian, Baaz

Monster Level 5, Medium Humanoid

Conditioned to be obedient and steady in combat, the perfect front-line soldier that stand their ground against enemy onslaught. Trained to excel when marching and fighting in formation and fight tactically, including using their death throes to turn the tide of battle.

| STR |  DEX   |   END   |  AWR   |  INT  |   CHA   |
| :-: | :----: | :-----: | :----: | :---: | :-----: |
|  3  |   1    |    2    |   0    |  -1   |    0    |
|  -  | REF 12 | FORT 12 | ANT 12 | LOG 9 | WILL 13 |

* Armor rating: 13 (Scale Mail 13, Heater shield +1 AR, +1 REF/ANT already added)
* Hit Points 30
* Speed 20'/Glide 40
* Traits:

  * Death Throes (Petrification): If brought to 0 HP the body turns to stone. If killed by an attack that pierces the body, the petrification can trap the weapon. Make +3 attack vs REF or ANT (defenders choice) - on hit the weapon is stuck. Anyone can use 2 AP to make an Athletics DC 13 to pull the weapon free. Until the petrified remains crumble, the tile it is on becomes impassable. Crumbles after 1d4 rounds leaving difficult TN on the tile.
  * Rallied by Dragons: Gains condition Rallied (cannot be Demoralized, Frightened, or Shaken) while allied dragons are present on the battle field.
  * Glide: Can glide 4 times the height they begin from.
  * Formation fighting: While within 5' of two other baaz, may target ANT instad of AR with melee attacks. This represents coordinated attacks and feints meant to confuse opponent.
* Actions: 2 from the below list. May choose the same twice.
  * Move
  * Short sword/spear/claw, +3 vs AR, d6+3/d6+2/d4+3 sharp
  * Cross bow/spear +3 vs AR, d6+1 200'/20'
  * Raise Shields: Gain +3 AR and physical defences until begining of next turn. This must be the last action taken this turn. Often used by the flank of the formation.
